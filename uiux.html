What is UI/UX Design?

UI (User Interface) Design focuses on the visual and interactive elements of a product, like buttons, icons, typography, and color schemes. It's about how the product looks and how users interact with its individual components. UX (User Experience) Design, on the other hand, is a broader field that encompasses the entire journey a user takes when interacting with a product, ensuring it's intuitive, efficient, and enjoyable. It involves research, information architecture, interaction design, and usability testing.
